# DATA LAWS

1. Data is classified as:
   - Raw Truth
   - Derived Knowledge
   - Decisions
   - Execution Records
   - Audit Evidence

2. Raw Truth is never altered.

3. Derived Knowledge must be reproducible from Raw Truth.

4. Decisions are immutable once recorded.

5. Deletions are forbidden except through legal retention policy.

6. All data must be attributable to:
   - Source
   - Time
   - Identity
