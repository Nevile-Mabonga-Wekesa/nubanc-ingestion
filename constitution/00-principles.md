# NUBANC - CORE PRINCIPLES

1. Replay Supremacy  
All system state must be reproducible from recorded events.

2. Immutability First  
Facts, decisions, and ledger entries are append-only.

3. Separation of Thinking and Acting  
Decision-making and execution must never share responsibilities.

4. Determinism  
Given identical inputs, the system must produce identical outputs.

5. Human Visibility, Not Human Dependence  
Humans may observe, approve, or halt-but not be required for correctness.

6. Cost Is a Signal  
Cost is treated as a first-class system metric.

7. No Silent Failure  
All failures must be observable and recorded.

8. Longevity Over Convenience  
Design choices favor decades of survivability over short-term speed.
